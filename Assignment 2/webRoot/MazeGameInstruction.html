Remember to install the emulator before you start playing the game.

How to play:

Select "Maze Game" or from the drop-down menu, and click "start".
Simply use your "Up, Down, Left, Right" key on the key board to move the rectangle.
The rectangle will not be able to pass the black line.
The only way to continue to the next level is reach the green hole in the centre.

Bugs & Problems

Sometimes the picture of the maze game will not show up.
Solution: All you need to do is double click the "start" button.
